About Plopquiz

We make smart quizzes for smart people.

Our quizzes are particularly smart because nobody writes them. Instead, they are crafted from open scholarly resources such as Wikipedia.

Businesses can use Plopquiz to screen job applicants before looking at a single resume, and to pro-actively attract well-qualified people for a position.

Quiz takers can use proficiency data to track personal learning performance, or find good career matches for their proficiencies. Proficiency data can be downloaded, or shared with other web services.
