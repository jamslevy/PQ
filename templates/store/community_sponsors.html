PlopQuiz community sponsors offer support for students learning about public interest issues such as energy and economic renewal.

Sponsorships can be earned by the top 10-15% of high-scoring students for a subject.

Upon earning a sponsorship, a student receives a plaque for their profile and a message from their new sponsor.

At any time, a community sponsor can:

  • Configure quiz subjects to support.
  • Customize a congratulatory message. Describe jobs openings, internships, or services offered by your organization.
  • Browse or export a list of sponsored students.
